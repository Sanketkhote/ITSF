Information Technocrats Student Forum

Vision

  • Achieving excellence in technical education for global acceptance

Mission

  • Make competent engineers with the spirit of professionalism and responsible citizenship
  • Impart knowledge and technical skills of the highest standards
  • Prepare engineers to respond to the current and future needs of the industry , higher studies as well as research

Objectives

  • The purpose of this organization is to provide an opportunity for students of ITSF
  • To improve a sense of community and unity
  • To provide platform to present their skills
  • To maintain academic freedom and promote leadership skills.

IT Department

  • Department of Information Technology was established in 2002, our aim is to provide our students the lifelong learning and leadership skills that enable them to grow in their professions and advance to positions of responsibility by effective industry-institute interaction.
  • Team of devoted faculties is the real strength of the department. Almost all staff members have published technical papers in various national, international journals and conferences.
  • Department is credited with consultancy services. Faculties are called for corporate training in various IT companies. Department has been well tuned in such a way that every faculty member gets self motivated and sets up a good example in front of the students.
